The π spigot

This is an image I made to visualize the digits of π.

The idea is to create a square grid, like a chess board, but inside each of the squares is a circle, the radius of which is determined by the digits of pi. As you can see in the 10 x 10 grid below, the digits of pi are given sequentially as you read down the columns. The colors are selected by combining the neighboring cells into an integer which indexes a palette.
